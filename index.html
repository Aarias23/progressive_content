Structure of Present Continuous Tense


Positive Form

Subject Be Verb + Ing complement
I am watching television
You are watching television
He is watching television
She is watching television
It is watching television
We are watching television
They are watching television

Negative Form

Subject Be + not Verb + Ing complement
I am not watching television
You are not watching television
He is not watching television
She is not watching television
It is not watching television
We are not watching television
They are not watching television

Question Form

Be Subject Verb + Ing complement
Am I watching television?
Are you watching television?
Is he watching television?
Is she watching television?
Is it watching television
Are we watching television?
Are they watching television?

Spelling Rules

Rules Examples
Most verb add ing Hold - Holding
read - reading
drink - drinking
Verbs ending in e erase the e and add ing take - taking
leave - leaving
Verbs ending in x, y, w add just add ing enjoy - enjoying
study - studying
Verbs that ends in consonant
vowel consonant and the
last syllable is stressed: double
the last consonant and add ing 		swim - swiming
